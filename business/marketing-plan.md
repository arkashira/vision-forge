 # marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
- One-liner: Vision-Forge: Accelerate Computer Vision Innovation with Efficient and Accurate Object Detection, Image Processing, and Tracking.
- Alternative 1: Vision-Forge Pro: Enterprise-Grade Computer Vision Platform for Scalable and Secure AI-Powered Vision Solutions.
- Alternative 2: Vision-Forge Lite: Affordable Computer Vision Starter Kit for Developers and Researchers to Build and Deploy AI-Powered Vision Applications.
- Alternative 3: Vision-Forge Edge: Lightweight and Powerful Computer Vision Edge Solution for Real-Time Object Detection and Tracking in IoT Devices.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
### Persona 1: Dr. Amelia Watson, Senior Computer Vision Researcher at XYZ Research Institute
- Daily Job: Leads a team of researchers in developing cutting-edge computer vision algorithms and applications.
- Budget: $100,000+ for annual computer vision platform subscriptions and research grants.

### Persona 2: John Lee, AI Engineer at ABC Corporation
- Daily Job: Develops and deploys computer vision applications for various industries, including retail, manufacturing, and transportation.
- Budget: $50,000+ for annual computer vision platform subscriptions and development resources.

### Persona 3: Sarah Kim, Founder of AI-Startup XYZ Vision
- Daily Job: Builds and scales AI-powered computer vision solutions for clients in various industries.
- Budget: $25,000+ for annual computer vision platform subscriptions and development resources.

## Channels
- Channel 1: LinkedIn Ads ($10,000 CAC estimate) - Targeted ads to AI engineers, data scientists, and computer vision researchers.
- Channel 2: Medium Publications ($5,000 CAC estimate) - Sponsored articles and guest posts on Medium publications focused on AI, computer vision, and machine learning.
- Channel 3: AI Conferences and Events ($3,000 CAC estimate) - Sponsorships, booths, and speaking engagements at AI conferences and events.

## Content Cadence
- Week 1: Launch teaser on LinkedIn, Medium, and Twitter.
- Week 2: Detailed product demo video and blog post.
- Week 3: Webinar featuring computer vision experts discussing the benefits of Vision-Forge.
- Week 4: Case study highlighting a successful project using Vision-Forge.
- Week 5: Live Q&A session with the Vision-Forge team.
- Ongoing: Weekly blog posts, newsletters, and social media updates.

## Launch Milestones
- D-30: Finalize marketing materials, secure conference sponsorships, and begin outreach to ICPs.
- D-0: Launch Vision-Forge, send press releases, and begin advertising campaigns.
- D+30: Evaluate initial customer feedback and adjust marketing strategy as needed.
- D+90: Assess DAU and $MRR targets, adjust pricing and marketing strategy as needed.

## Success Metrics
- DAU (Daily Active Users): 1,000 by D+30, 2,000 by D+60, 3,000 by D+90.
- $MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) Target by D+90: $50,000.
