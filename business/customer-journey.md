# Customer Journey Map: vision-forge

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Developer/researcher encounters a need for advanced computer vision capabilities.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness of available solutions, unclear differentiation between existing platforms.
- **User Emotions**: Curiosity, uncertainty.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide educational content, case studies, webinars.
- **Metric**: Number of website visitors, engagement with marketing content.

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Researching computer vision platforms, comparing features and pricing.
- **Friction Points**: Complex pricing models, unclear ROI, lack of community support.
- **User Emotions**: Skepticism, frustration.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer free trials, detailed documentation, responsive customer support.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from website visitors to trial sign-ups.

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: Signing up for a free trial, integrating the platform into a project.
- **Friction Points**: Integration challenges, learning curve, technical issues.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement, frustration.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide onboarding support, tutorials, community forums.
- **Metric**: Trial completion rate, customer satisfaction scores.

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Deciding to purchase the platform, integrating it into production.
- **Friction Points**: Cost, scalability issues, lack of advanced features.
- **User Emotions**: Relief, satisfaction.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer custom solutions, scalability support, advanced feature training.
- **Metric**: Purchase conversion rate, customer lifetime value.

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Using the platform extensively, exploring additional features.
- **Friction Points**: Limited customization, lack of integration with other tools.
- **User Emotions**: Engagement, satisfaction.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide custom development services, integration support, advanced training.
- **Metric**: Customer retention rate, upsell opportunities.
